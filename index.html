Challenging History Riddles

1. I ruled without a crown, fought without a sword, and my strongest weapon was silence. Who am I?

2. I am the ancient path traders walked, carrying silk and ideas. What route am I?

3. My fall marked the end of an empire in 1453. What city am I?

4. I am the script no one has fully read, found on seals of an ancient civilization. What script am I?

Difficult Geography Riddles

1. I move without legs, shape continents, and swallow cities, yet I sleep for centuries. What am I?

2. I am a place where no rain falls, yet snow lies forever. Where am I?

3. I am a line that decides time but not seasons. What line am I?

4. I am a river that flows backwards compared to all others in my country. What river am I?
